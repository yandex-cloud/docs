The `managed-kafka.maintenanceTask.editor` role enables viewing info on [maintenance](../../../managed-kafka/concepts/maintenance.md) tasks for {{ KF }} clusters and modifying such tasks, as well as viewing info on [{{ KF }} clusters](../../../managed-kafka/concepts/index.md), [access permissions](../../../iam/concepts/access-control/index.md) granted for them, and on [quotas](../../../managed-kafka/concepts/limits.md#mkf-quotas) and resource operations for {{ mkf-name }}.

This role includes the `managed-kafka.maintenanceTask.viewer` permissions.
