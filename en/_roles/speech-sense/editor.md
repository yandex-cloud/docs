
The `speech-sense.editor` role enables you to edit a [project]({{ link-docs-ai }}speechsense/concepts/resources-hierarchy#project), its description, dashboards, and alerts, create and edit its classifiers, and run analyses. When assigned for a [space]({{ link-docs-ai }}speechsense/concepts/resources-hierarchy#space), the role allows you to edit the space and create projects, [connections]({{ link-docs-ai }}speechsense/concepts/resources-hierarchy#connection), and [dictionaries]({{ link-docs-ai }}speechsense/concepts/dictionaries) within it.

This role includes the `speech-sense.viewer` and `speech-sense.spaces.creator` permissions.
