The `speech-sense.editor` role enables you to edit a [project](../../speechsense/concepts/resources-hierarchy.md#project), its description, dashboards, and alerts, create and edit its classifiers, and run analyses. When assigned for a [space](../../speechsense/concepts/resources-hierarchy.md#space), the role allows you to edit the space and create projects, [connections](../../speechsense/concepts/resources-hierarchy.md#connection), and [dictionaries](../../speechsense/concepts/dictionaries.md) within it.

This role includes the `speech-sense.viewer` and `speech-sense.spaces.creator` permissions.
