
The `speech-sense.admin` role assigned for a [space]({{ link-docs-ai }}speechsense/concepts/resources-hierarchy#space) or [project]({{ link-docs-ai }}speechsense/concepts/resources-hierarchy#project) enables you to perform any action in them: view [dialogs]({{ link-docs-ai }}speechsense/concepts/dialogs), edit [connections]({{ link-docs-ai }}speechsense/concepts/resources-hierarchy#connection), or run [analyses]({{ link-docs-ai }}speechsense/concepts/assistants#analysis). The role grants permission to assign roles to other users.

This role includes the `speech-sense.editor` and `speech-sense.data.editor` permissions.
