The `speech-sense.admin` role assigned for a [space](../../speechsense/concepts/resources-hierarchy.md#space) or [project](../../speechsense/concepts/resources-hierarchy.md#project) enables you to perform any action in them: view [dialogs](../../speechsense/concepts/dialogs.md), edit [connections](../../speechsense/concepts/resources-hierarchy.md#connection), or run [analyses](../../speechsense/concepts/assistants.md#analysis). The role grants permission to assign roles to other users.

This role includes the `speech-sense.editor` and `speech-sense.data.editor` permissions.
