The `speech-sense.data.viewer` role allows you to view a project's name or description, the list of [connections](../../../speechsense/concepts/resources-hierarchy.md#connection), dashboards, and [project](../../../speechsense/concepts/resources-hierarchy.md#project) members. It also enables you to search inside documents, listen to [dialogs](../../../speechsense/concepts/dialogs.md), and view their text [transcripts](../../../speechsense/concepts/dialogs.md#contents). When assigned for a [space](../../../speechsense/concepts/resources-hierarchy.md#space), this role enables you to view all of its projects without editing them.
