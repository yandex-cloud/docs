The `managed-airflow.viewer` role enables viewing info on [{{ AF }} clusters](../../managed-airflow/concepts/index.md#cluster), [access permissions](../../iam/concepts/access-control/index.md) granted for them, and their [maintenance](../../managed-airflow/concepts/maintenance.md) tasks, as well as on [quotas](../../managed-airflow/concepts/limits.md#quotas) for {{ maf-name }}.

This role includes the `managed-airflow.auditor` and `managed-airflow.maintenanceTask.viewer` permissions.
