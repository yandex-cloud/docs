The `managed-airflow.maintenanceTask.editor` role enables viewing info on [maintenance](../../../managed-airflow/concepts/maintenance.md) tasks for Apache Airflow™ clusters and modifying such tasks, as well as viewing info on [Apache Airflow™ clusters](../../../managed-airflow/concepts/index.md#cluster), [access permissions](../../../iam/concepts/access-control/index.md) granted for them, and on [quotas](../../../managed-airflow/concepts/limits.md#quotas) for Managed Service for Apache Airflow™.

This role includes the `managed-airflow.maintenanceTask.viewer` permissions.
