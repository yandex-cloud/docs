The `organization-manager.groups.viewer` role enables viewing info on [user groups](../../../organization/concepts/groups.md) and [access permissions](../../../iam/concepts/access-control/index.md) granted for them, as well as viewing the list of [users](../../../overview/roles-and-resources.md#users) and [service accounts](../../../iam/concepts/users/service-accounts.md) that are members of groups.
