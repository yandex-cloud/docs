The `editor` role provides permissions to manage any Yandex Cloud [resources]({{ link-docs }}/resource-manager/concepts/resources-hierarchy), except for assigning roles to other users, transferring [organization]({{ link-docs }}/organization/concepts/organization) ownership, removing an organization, and deleting Key Management Service [encryption keys]({{ link-docs }}/kms/concepts/).

For instance, users with this role can create, modify, and delete resources.

This role includes the `viewer` permissions.
