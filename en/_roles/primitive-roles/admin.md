The `admin` role enables assigning any roles, except for `resource-manager.clouds.owner` and `organization-manager.organizations.owner`, and provides permissions to manage any Yandex Cloud [resources]({{ link-docs }}/resource-manager/concepts/resources-hierarchy) (except for transferring [organization]({{ link-docs }}/organization/concepts/organization) ownership and removing an organization).

Prior to assigning the `admin` role for an organization, [cloud]({{ link-docs }}/resource-manager/concepts/resources-hierarchy#cloud), or [billing account]({{ link-docs }}/billing/concepts/billing-account), make sure to check out the information on protecting [privileged accounts]({{ link-docs }}/security/standard/all#privileged-users).

This role includes the `editor` permissions.
