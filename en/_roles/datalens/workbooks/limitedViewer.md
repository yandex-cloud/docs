You can assign the `datalens.workbooks.limitedViewer` role to a [workbook](../../../datalens/workbooks-collections/index.md). With it, you can view all the workbook's nested [charts](../../../datalens/concepts/chart/index.md), [dashboards](../../../datalens/concepts/dashboard.md), and [reports](../../../datalens/reports/index.md), as well as info on the [access permissions](../../../iam/concepts/access-control/index.md) granted for the workbook. In the {{ datalens-name }} UI, this role is referred to as `Limited viewer`. You may want to only assign this role through the {{ datalens-name }} UI.
