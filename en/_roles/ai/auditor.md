The `ai.auditor` role enables viewing info on quotas for [Yandex Translate]({{ link-docs-ai }}translate/concepts/limits#translate-quotas), [Yandex Vision OCR]({{ link-docs-ai }}vision/concepts/limits#vision-quotas), [Yandex SpeechKit]({{ link-docs-ai }}speechkit/concepts/limits#speechkit-quotas), and [Yandex AI Studio]({{ link-docs-ai }}ai-studio/concepts/limits#yandexgpt-quotas), on uploaded [files]({{ link-docs-ai }}ai-studio/concepts/search/vectorstore#file-uploading), Vector Store [search indexes]({{ link-docs-ai }}ai-studio/concepts/search/vectorstore), [datasets]({{ link-docs-ai }}ai-studio/concepts/resources/dataset), and [text generation models]({{ link-docs-ai }}ai-studio/concepts/generation/models) in Yandex AI Studio, view metadata on [guardrails]({{ link-docs-ai }}ai-studio/concepts/security/guardrails#rules) for model responses, as well as info on the relevant [cloud]({{ link-docs }}/resource-manager/concepts/resources-hierarchy#cloud) and [folder]({{ link-docs }}/resource-manager/concepts/resources-hierarchy#folder).

This role includes the `ai.assistants.auditor`, `ai.datasets.auditor`, `ai.models.auditor`, and `ai.guardrails.auditor` permissions.
