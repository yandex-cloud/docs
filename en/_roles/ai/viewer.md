The `ai.viewer` role enables viewing info on quotas for [{{ translate-full-name }}]({{ link-docs-ai }}translate/concepts/limits#translate-quotas), [{{ vision-full-name }}]({{ link-docs-ai }}vision/concepts/limits#vision-quotas), [{{ speechkit-full-name }}]({{ link-docs-ai }}speechkit/concepts/limits#speechkit-quotas), and [{{ ai-studio-full-name }}]({{ link-docs-ai }}ai-studio/concepts/limits#yandexgpt-quotas), on [text generation models]({{ link-docs-ai }}ai-studio/concepts/generation/models), [guardrails]({{ link-docs-ai }}ai-studio/concepts/security/guardrails#rules) for model responses, [datasets]({{ link-docs-ai }}ai-studio/concepts/resources/dataset), uploaded [files]({{ link-docs-ai }}ai-studio/concepts/search/vectorstore#file-uploading), and {{ vector-store-name }} [search indexes]({{ link-docs-ai }}ai-studio/concepts/search/vectorstore) in {{ ai-studio-full-name }}, read such files and conduct searches using these indexes, as well as view info on the relevant [cloud]({{ link-docs }}/resource-manager/concepts/resources-hierarchy#cloud) and [folder]({{ link-docs }}/resource-manager/concepts/resources-hierarchy#folder).

This role includes the `ai.auditor`, `ai.assistants.viewer`, `ai.datasets.viewer`, `ai.models.viewer`, and `ai.guardrails.viewer` permissions.
