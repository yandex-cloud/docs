The `ai.viewer` role enables viewing info on quotas for [Yandex Translate](../../translate/concepts/limits.md#translate-quotas), [Yandex Vision OCR](../../vision/concepts/limits.md#vision-quotas), [Yandex SpeechKit](../../speechkit/concepts/limits.md#speechkit-quotas), and [Yandex AI Studio](../../ai-studio/concepts/limits.md#yandexgpt-quotas), on [text generation models](../../ai-studio/concepts/generation/models.md), [datasets](../../ai-studio/concepts/resources/dataset.md), uploaded [files](../../ai-studio/concepts/search/vectorstore.md#file-uploading), and Vector Store [search indexes](../../ai-studio/concepts/search/vectorstore.md) in Yandex AI Studio, read such files and conduct searches using such indexes, as well view info on the relevant [cloud](../../resource-manager/concepts/resources-hierarchy.md#cloud) and [folder](../../resource-manager/concepts/resources-hierarchy.md#folder).

This role includes the `ai.auditor`, `ai.assistants.viewer`, `ai.datasets.viewer`, and `ai.models.viewer` permissions.
