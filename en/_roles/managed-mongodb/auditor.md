The `managed-mongodb.auditor` role enables viewing info on [Yandex StoreDoc clusters](../../storedoc/concepts/index.md) and [access permissions](../../iam/concepts/access-control/index.md) granted for them, on [hosts](../../storedoc/concepts/instance-types.md) and cluster [backups](../../storedoc/concepts/backup.md), and on [quotas](../../storedoc/concepts/limits.md#mmg-quotas) and resource operations.
