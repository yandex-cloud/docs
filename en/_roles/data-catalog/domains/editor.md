The `data-catalog.domains.editor` role enables viewing info on domains in {{ data-catalog-name }} and managing them.

Users with this role can:
* View info on [domains](../../../iam/concepts/access-control/index.md) in {{ data-catalog-name }} as well as creating, using, modifying, and deleting them.
* View info on [access permissions](../../../iam/concepts/access-control/index.md) granted for domains in {{ data-catalog-name }}.

This role includes the `data-catalog.domains.viewer` and `data-catalog.domains.user` permissions.
