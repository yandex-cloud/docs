The `data-catalog.domains.admin` role enables managing domains and access to them in {{ data-catalog-name }}.

Users with this role can:
* View info on [access permissions](../../../iam/concepts/access-control/index.md) granted for domains in {{ data-catalog-name }} and modify such permissions.
* View info on [domains](../../../iam/concepts/access-control/index.md) in {{ data-catalog-name }} as well as create, use, modify, and delete them.

This role includes the `data-catalog.domains.editor` permissions.
