The `data-catalog.catalogs.editor` role enables viewing info on catalogs in {{ data-catalog-name }} and managing them.

Users with this role can:
* View info on catalogs in {{ data-catalog-name }} as well as create, modify, and delete them.
* View info on [access permissions](../../../iam/concepts/access-control/index.md) granted to catalogs in {{ data-catalog-name }}.
* View info on the {{ data-catalog-name }} [quotes](../../../metadata-hub/concepts/limits.md#data-catalog-quota).

This role includes the `data-catalog.catalogs.viewer` permissions.
