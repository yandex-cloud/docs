The `data-catalog.catalogs.viewer` role enables viewing info on catalogs in {{ data-catalog-name }}, [access permissions](../../../iam/concepts/access-control/index.md) granted for them, and the {{ data-catalog-name }} [quotes](../../../metadata-hub/concepts/limits.md#data-catalog-quota).

This role includes the `data-catalog.catalogs.auditor` permissions.
