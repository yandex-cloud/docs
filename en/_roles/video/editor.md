
The `video.editor` enables managing {{ video-name }} resources or a dedicated channel’s resources, as well as broadcasting video streams.

Users with this role can:
* View info on {{ video-name }} resources and their settings, as well as create, modify, and delete such resources.
* [Broadcast](../../video/concepts/streams.md#streams) live video streams from {{ video-name }}.
* Download source [video](../../video/concepts/videos.md) and [subtitle](../../video/concepts/videos.md#subtitles) files as well as thumbnails.
* Use AI features, such as video [summarization](../../video/concepts/videos.md#summarization) and [neural machine translation](../../video/concepts/videos.md#stranslation).
* View info on [access permissions](../../iam/concepts/access-control/index.md) granted for {{ video-name }} [channels](../../video/concepts/index.md#channels).

This role includes the `video.viewer` permissions.
