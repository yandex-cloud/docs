The `managed-spark.maintenanceTask.viewer` role enables viewing info on [{{ SPRK }} clusters](../../../managed-spark/concepts/index.md), [access permissions](../../../iam/concepts/access-control/index.md) granted for them, their [maintenance tasks](../../../managed-spark/concepts/maintenance.md), and on [quotas](../../../managed-spark/concepts/limits.md#quotas) for {{ msp-name }}.

This role includes the `managed-spark.auditor` permissions.
