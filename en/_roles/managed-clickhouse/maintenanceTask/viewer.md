The `managed-clickhouse.maintenanceTask.viewer` role enables viewing info on [ClickHouse® clusters](../../../managed-clickhouse/concepts/index.md), their [maintenance tasks](../../../managed-clickhouse/concepts/maintenance.md), [access permissions](../../../iam/concepts/access-control/index.md) granted for them, and on [quotas](../../../managed-clickhouse/concepts/limits.md#mch-quotas) and resource operations for Managed Service for ClickHouse®.

This role includes the `managed-clickhouse.auditor` permissions.
