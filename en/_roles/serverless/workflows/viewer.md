The `serverless.workflows.viewer` role enables viewing info on [workflows](../../../serverless-integrations/concepts/limits.md#workflows) and [access permissions](../../../iam/concepts/access-control/index.md) assigned to them, viewing the history of workflow [executions](../../../serverless-integrations/concepts/limits.md#workflows), as well as info on Yandex Workflows [quotas](../../../serverless-integrations/concepts/limits.md#workflows).

This role includes the `serverless.workflows.auditor` permissions.
