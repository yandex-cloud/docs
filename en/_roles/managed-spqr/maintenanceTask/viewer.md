The `managed-spqr.maintenanceTask.viewer` role enables viewing info on [Sharded PostgreSQL clusters](../../../managed-spqr/concepts/index.md), their maintenance tasks, [access permissions](../../../iam/concepts/access-control/index.md) granted for them, [hosts](../../../managed-spqr/concepts/instance-types.md), and on [quotas](../../../managed-spqr/concepts/limits.md#mspqr-quotas) and resource operations for Managed Service for Sharded PostgreSQL.

This role includes the `managed-spqr.auditor` permissions.
