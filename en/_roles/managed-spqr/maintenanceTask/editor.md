The `managed-spqr.maintenanceTask.editor` role enables viewing info on maintenance tasks for [{{ SPQR }} clusters](../../../managed-spqr/concepts/index.md) and modifying such tasks, as well as viewing info on {{ SPQR }} clusters, [access permissions](../../../iam/concepts/access-control/index.md) granted for them, cluster [hosts](../../../managed-spqr/concepts/instance-types.md), [quotas](../../../managed-spqr/concepts/limits.md#mspqr-quotas), and resource operations for {{ mspqr-name }}.

This role includes the `managed-spqr.maintenanceTask.viewer` permissions.
