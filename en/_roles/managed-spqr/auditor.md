The `managed-spqr.auditor` role enables viewing info on Sharded PostgreSQL [hosts](../../managed-spqr/concepts/instance-types.md) and [clusters](../../managed-spqr/concepts/index.md), on [access permissions](../../iam/concepts/access-control/index.md) granted for clusters, as well as on [quotas](../../managed-spqr/concepts/limits.md#mspqr-quotas) and resource operations for Managed Service for Sharded PostgreSQL.
