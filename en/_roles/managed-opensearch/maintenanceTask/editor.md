The `managed-opensearch.maintenanceTask.editor` role enables viewing info on [maintenance](../../../managed-opensearch/concepts/maintenance.md) tasks for OpenSearch clusters and modifying such tasks, as well as viewing info on [OpenSearch clusters](../../../managed-opensearch/concepts/index.md), [access permissions](../../../iam/concepts/access-control/index.md) granted for them, and on [quotas](../../../managed-opensearch/concepts/limits.md#quotas) and resource operations for Managed Service for OpenSearch.

This role includes the `managed-opensearch.maintenanceTask.viewer` permissions.
