The `monium.mutes.editor` role enables viewing, creating, editing, and deleting [mutes](../../../monium/alerts/mutes.md), i.e., rules for temporarily disabling [alert](../../../monium/concepts/alerting/alert.md) [notifications](../../../monium/concepts/alerting/notification-channel.md).

This role includes the `monium.mutes.viewer` permissions.
