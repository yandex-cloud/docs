The `monium.telemetry.reader` role enables reading all types of {{ monium-name }} telemetry, such as [metrics](../../../monium/metrics/quickstart.md), [logs](../../../monium/logs/quickstart.md), and [distributed tracing](../../../monium/traces/index.md).

This role includes the `monium.metrics.reader`, `monium.logs.reader`, and `monium.traces.reader` permissions.
