  By default, you get data for the current month. To change this period, click ![image](../../_assets/console-icons/calendar.svg) and set the **{{ ui-key.yacloud_billing.billing.account.detail.label_from }}** and **{{ ui-key.yacloud_billing.billing.account.detail.label_to }}** dates or select a period. A date is defined as an interval from 0:00 to 23:59 (UTC+3). Data may be updated with a slight delay (up to several hours).
