# How to change the email address



## Case description {#case-description}

You need to change the email address for notifications and document delivery.

## Solution {#case-resolution}

You can change your postal and email address on the **{{ ui-key.yacloud_billing.billing.account.create-new.section_persons }}** tab of the [**Yandex Balance**](https://balance.yandex.com) page or in your [management console]({{ link-console-settings }}) settings.
