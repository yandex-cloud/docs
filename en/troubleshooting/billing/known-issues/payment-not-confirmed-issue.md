# Billing account has the `PAYMENT_NOT_CONFIRMED` status



## Issue description {#issue-description}

* The billing account has the `PAYMENT_NOT_CONFIRMED` status.
* You cannot use the billing account and create new resources in the cloud linked to it.

## Solution {#issue-resolution}

The `PAYMENT_NOT_CONFIRMED` status means that you need to confirm your billing account.

Email the following documents to [{{ billing-docs-email }}](mailto:{{ billing-docs-email }}):
   * Copy of the minutes or resolution on the appointment of the general director.
   * [Billing account](../../../billing/concepts/billing-account.md#billing-account-id) ID.

Once your documents have been verified, you can activate your billing account and start using {{ yandex-cloud }}.

## If the issue persists {#if-issue-still-persists}

If the above actions did not help, [create a support ticket]({{ link-console-support }}). Provide the following information in your ticket:


1. ID of the billing account that has the `PAYMENT_NOT_CONFIRMED` status.  You can find it on the [page with your billing account details]({{ link-console-billing }}).
1. Issue description.
