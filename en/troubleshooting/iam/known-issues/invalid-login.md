# Resolving the `Invalid login` error


## Issue description {#issue-description}

When logging into the [management console]({{ link-console-main }}) via Yandex ID, the `Invalid login` message appears.

## Solution {#issue-resolution}

Complete your registration first, and then log back into your [management console]({{ link-console-main }}). Follow our step-by-step guide [here](https://yandex.com/support/id/en/authorization/lite.html#lite__login-yandex).
