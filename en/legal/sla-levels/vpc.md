# Service Level for Virtual Private Cloud


This document defines Service Level of Virtual Private Cloud ("Service") and is an integral part of Nebius Customer Agreement ("Agreement") and SLA available at: <https://nebius.com/il/docs/legal/sla>. Capitalized terms used herein but not defined herein shall have the meanings set forth in Agreement and Linked Documents.

| Service Level | 99.50% |
| --- | --- |

## Compensation Amount {#compensation-amount}

Availability of Platform cloud resources connected to Filtering Incoming Traffic

| Service Uptime Percentage in Reporting Period | Compensation, % of Service Fee for Filtering Incoming Traffic in Reporting Period |
| --- | --- |
| < 99.50%-99.00% | 10.0% |
| < 99.00%-95.00% | 15.0% |
| less than 95.00% | 30.0% |

## Definition of Service Unavailability {#unavailability}

Loss of external connectiveness of Platform's cloud resources used by Customer with the public IP address provided by Nebius in respect of the service of Filtering Incoming Traffic was provided.
