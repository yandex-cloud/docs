# Tickets from Lunapark

[Lunapark](http://lunapark.yandex-team.ru/) is an internal service for load testing.

To display a link to a ticket or another object from Lunapark on a {{wiki-name}} page, copy its URL from the browser address bar. For example:
* `http://lunapark.yandex-team.ru/MAPSRENDER-308`
* `http://lunapark.yandex-team.ru/100`

As a result, a magic link to the respective Lunapark ticket or object appears on the page:
