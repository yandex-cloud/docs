# Marking pages as outdated

If a page contains obsolete information, you can mark the page as outdated:

1. Open the page.

1. In the upper-right corner of the page, click ![](../_assets/wiki/svg/actions-icon.svg) **Actions** and select ![](../_assets/wiki/svg/current.svg) **Mark as outdated**.

1. If necessary, leave a comment and specify a link to the page with up-to-date information.

1. Click **Confirm**.

## Removing the "Outdated" mark from a page {#delete-tag}

If a page has been updated, you can remove the **page was marked as obsolete** mark:

1. In the **page was marked as obsolete** mark, click **It is actual**.

1. If necessary, leave a comment and click **Confirm**.
