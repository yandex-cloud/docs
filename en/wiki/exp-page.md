# Marking pages as outdated

If a page contains obsolete information, you can mark the page as outdated:

1. Open a page.

1. In the upper-right corner of the page, click ![](../_assets/wiki/ico-actions.png) **Action** and choose **Mark as "Outdated"** (for dynamic tables, ![](../_assets/wiki/table-settings-footer.png) â†’ **Mark as <q>Outdated</q>**).

1. You can leave a comment with a link to the up-to-date page if necessary.

1. Click **Save**.

## Unmarking outdated pages {#delete-tag}

If a page has been updated, you can remove the **Outdated** label:

1. Click on the**Outdated** label.

1. Click **Unmark**.

1. Leave a comment if necessary and click **Save**.
