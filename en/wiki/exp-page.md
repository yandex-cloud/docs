# Marking outdated pages

If a {{ wiki-name }} page is outdated, you can mark it as such:

1. Open a page.

1. In the upper-right corner of the page, click **Actions** and select **Mark as outdated** (for dynamic tables ![](../_assets/wiki/table-settings-footer.png) â†’ **Mark as <q>outdated</q>**).

1. You can leave a comment with a link to the updated page if necessary.

1. Click **Save**.

## Unmarking outdated pages {#delete-tag}

If the information on the page has become relevant again, you can remove the **Outdated page** label:

1. Click **Outdated page**.

1. Click **Unmark**.

1. Click **Save**. You can leave a comment if necessary.
