# Marking obsolete pages

If the information on a page is outdated, you can mark it as outdated:

1. Open the page.

1. In the upper-right corner of the page, click ![](../_assets/wiki/svg/actions-icon.svg) **Actions** and select **Mark as outdated** (for dynamic tables ![](../_assets/wiki/table-settings-footer.png) â†’ **Mark as <q>outdated</q>**).

1. If necessary, leave a comment and a link to the page with up-to-date information.

1. Click **Save**.

## Unmarking outdated pages{#delete-tag}

If the information on the page has become relevant again, you can remove the **Outdated data** label:

1. Click **Outdated data**.

1. Click **Unmark**.

1. Click **Save**. You can leave a comment if necessary.
