# Search by {{ wiki-name }}

To search text in Wiki pages:

1. In the left panel {{ wiki-name }}, click ![](../_assets/wiki/svg/fav.svg) **Search**.

1. Enter your query in the search bar. The Wiki pages that contain the specified text will be shown under the search bar.



1. To refine the search results, in the search bar, press **Enter** and use the filters in the right panel:
   - **Cluster**. The URL of the [cluster](structure.md#clusters) relative to the homepage `{{ wiki-host-name }}`. For example, to find text on subpages of the cluster `{{ wiki-username }}`, enter `users/userName`.
   - **Page author**: The name or username of the {{ wiki-name }} page author.
   - **Created** or **Updated**: The date when the page was created or last changed.
   - **Document type**: Search for a specific type of document {{ wiki-name }}: page, table, or file.



1. Sort the search results: by relevance, date modified, or creation date.




{% note warning %}

Recent changes to Wiki pages may appear in search results after a delay, since the pages are indexed once every 30 minutes.

{% endnote %}
