# Search {{ wiki-name }}

To search the text of {{ wiki-name }} pages and [keywords](add-key-words.md), use the search bar that appears in the upper-right part of each page: enter a query and click ![](../_assets/wiki/search-icon.png) or press **Enter**.

## Possible problems

- Recent changes to {{ wiki-name }} pages may appear in search results after a delay, since the pages are indexed once every 30 minutes.

- The search bar on {{ wiki-name }} does not work if you are a member of multiple [Yandex.Connect organizations]({{ support-enable-connect }}).

    In this case, use the search on the Yandex.Connect main page:

    1. Go to [Yandex.Connect]({{ link-connect }}).

    1. Select an organization from the drop-down list.

    1. Enter your query in the search bar and click ![](../_assets/wiki/search-icon.png) or press **Enter**.

    1. On the search results page, in the panel on the left, select the **Wiki** tab.
