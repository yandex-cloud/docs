# Allow tracking cookies

If a document from Office 365 is not displayed on a {{wiki-name}} page, your browser might be blocking cross-site tracking cookies (trackers) that are used to track user actions on third-party sites.

{{wiki-name}} uses cross-site tracking cookies to access cloud documents on the Office 365 portal. To view cloud documents on Wiki pages, disable the ban on using tracking cookies.

## What are tracking cookies (trackers)? {#what-is-tracking}

Trackers (third-party cookies) are small chunks of data that are saved to your computer when you open a website. Cookies store information about the user and their actions (such as location, type of device, or IP address) and transmit it to the website.

Trackers may be used in good faith (for example, to collect statistics) and in bad faith (for example, to collect and transfer user personal data). Most modern browsers have an option that allows you to block trackers that are not playing fair.

To ensure that documents from Office 365 are displayed properly, try to disable tracker blocking.

## How to disable blocking of tracking cookies {#off-tracking}

### Safari {#safari}

[Full version](https://support.apple.com/ru-ru/guide/safari/sfri40732/mac):

1. In the browser settings, select **Privacy**.

1. Disable **Prevent cross-site tracking**.

[Mobile version](https://support.apple.com/ru-ru/guide/iphone/iphb01fc3c85/ios):

1. Open **Preferences** → **Safari**.

1. Open **Privacy & Security** and disable **Prevent cross-site tracking**.

### Mozilla Firefox {#firefox}

[Full version](https://support.mozilla.org/ru/kb/uluchshennaya-zashita-ot-otslezhivaniya-v-firefox-#w_kak-uznat-chto-blokiruetsia-na-saite_2):

On the page with a {{wiki-name}} document, click ![](../../_assets/wiki/mozilla-security.png) and turn the **Enhanced tracking protection is ON for this site** toggle off.

[Mobile version](https://support.mozilla.org/ru/kb/zashita-ot-otslezhivaniya-v-firefox-dlya-android#w_izmenenie-urovnia-zashchity):

In the browser settings, disable **Enhanced tracking protection**.

### Google Chrome {#chrome}

[Full version](https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/2790761?co=GENIE.Platform%3DDesktop&hl=ru&oco=1):

1. In the browser settings, select **Privacy and security**, click **Cookies and other site data** or follow the `chrome://settings/cookies` link.

1. Turn **Send a "Do not track" request with your browsing traffic** off.

[Mobile version (Android)](https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/2790761?co=GENIE.Platform%3DAndroid&hl=ru&oco=1):

1. In the browser settings, tap **Privacy and security** → **Do Not Track**.

1. Turn the setting **Off**.

### Yandex&#160;Browser {#ya}

[Full version](https://yandex.ru/support/browser/personal-data-protection/ytp.html#blocking-by-settings):

* Unblock a specific tracker:

   1. Click ![](../../_assets/wiki/ya-security.png) in the SmartBox.

   1. To the right of the **sharepoint.com** and **yastatic.net** trackers, click ![](../../_assets/wiki/ico-actions.png) → **Unblock**.

* Allow trackers for all sites:

   1. In the browser settings, select **Sites** → **Advanced site settings**.

   1. Under "Cookies", select the **Restrict third-party cookies** blocking option to allow only the websites you visited to use trackers.

[Mobile version](https://yandex.ru/support/browser-mobile-android-phone/privacy/ytp.html#unblocking):

1. Click ![](../../_assets/wiki/ya-security.png) in the SmartBox.

1. Under "Tracker blocking", tap "Site not working?".

1. In the dialog box that appears, tap **Allow cookies**.

1. Reload the page.
