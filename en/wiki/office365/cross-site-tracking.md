# Enabling tracking cookies

If an Office 365 document isn't displayed in {{ wiki-name }}, your browser might have cross-site cookies (trackers) disabled. They are used for tracking user actions on third-party sites.

{{ wiki-name }} uses cross-site tracking cookies to access cloud documents hosted on Office 365. To view cloud documents on Wiki pages, allow cross-site tracking cookies.

## What are tracking cookies (trackers)? {#what-is-tracking}

Trackers (third-party cookies) are small data fragments saved to your PC when you open a website. Cookies store information about a user and their actions (such as their location, device type, or IP address) and transfer it to the website.

Trackers can be used in good faith (such as to collect statistics) and in bad faith (such as to collect and transfer user personal data). Most modern browsers allow you to block malicious trackers.

To make sure Office 365 documents are displayed properly, try to disable tracker blocking.

## How to disable blocking cross-site tracking cookies {#off-tracking}

### Safari {#safari}

[Desktop version](https://support.apple.com/ru-ru/guide/safari/sfri40732/mac):

1. In your browser settings, select **Privacy**.

1. Turn off **Prevent cross-site tracking**.


[Mobile version](https://support.apple.com/ru-ru/guide/iphone/iphb01fc3c85/ios):

1. Select **Preferences** → **Safari**.

1. Open **Privacy & Security** and turn off **Prevent cross-site tracking**.

### Mozilla Firefox {#firefox}

[Desktop version](https://support.mozilla.org/ru/kb/uluchshennaya-zashita-ot-otslezhivaniya-v-firefox-#w_kak-uznat-chto-blokiruetsia-na-saite_2):

On the document page in {{ wiki-name }}, click ![](../../_assets/wiki/mozilla-security.png) and turn off **Enhanced tracking protection is ON for this site**.

[Mobile version](https://support.mozilla.org/ru/kb/zashita-ot-otslezhivaniya-v-firefox-dlya-android#w_izmenenie-urovnia-zashchity):

In the browser settings, turn off **Enhanced tracking protection**.

### Google Chrome {#chrome}

[Desktop version](https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/2790761?co=GENIE.Platform%3DDesktop&hl=ru&oco=1):

1. In the browser settings, click **Privacy and security** and then **Cookies and other site data** or follow the link `chrome://settings/cookies`.

1. Turn off **Send a "Do Not Track" request with your browsing traffic**.

[Mobile version for Android](https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/2790761?co=GENIE.Platform%3DAndroid&hl=ru&oco=1):
1. In the browser settings, tap **Privacy and security** → **Do Not Track**.

1. Turn the setting **Off**.

### Yandex&#160;Browser {#ya}

[Desktop version](https://yandex.ru/support/browser/personal-data-protection/ytp.html#blocking-by-settings):

* Unblock a specific tracker:

   1. Click ![](../../_assets/wiki/ya-security.png) in the SmartBox.

   1. Select ![](../../_assets/wiki/ico-actions.png) → **Unblock** next to the **sharepoint.com** and **yastatic.net** trackers.

* Allow trackers for all sites:

   1. In the browser settings, select **Sites** → **Advanced site settings**.

   1. Under "Cookies", select **Restrict third-party cookies** to only allow them to be used by websites that you've visited before.

[Mobile version](https://yandex.ru/support/browser-mobile-android-phone/privacy/ytp.html#unblocking):

1. Tap ![](../../_assets/wiki/ya-security.png) in the SmartBox.

1. Under "Tracker blocking", tap "Site not working?".

1. In the window that opens, tap **Allow cookies**.

1. Refresh the page.
