# Allow tracking cookies

If an Office 365 document is not displayed on the page in {{wiki-name}}, your browser might be blocking cross-site cookies (trackers) used for tracking user action on third-party sites.

{{wiki-name}} uses cross-site cookies to access cloud documents on the Office 365 portal. To view cloud documents on Wiki pages, disable restrictions on tracking cookies.

## What are tracking cookies (trackers)? {#what-is-tracking}

Trackers (third-party cookies) are small pieces of data that are saved to your computer when you open a website. Cookies store information about users and their actions (such as location, type of device, or IP addresses) and send it to the site.

Trackers may be used in good faith (for example, to collect statistics) or in bad faith (for example, to collect and distribute users' personal data). In most modern browsers, you can block malicious trackers.

To make sure that Office 365 documents are displayed correctly, try to disable tracker blocking.

## How to enable tracking cookies {#off-tracking}

### Safari {#safari}

[Full version](https://support.apple.com/ru-ru/guide/safari/sfri40732/mac):

1. Select **Privacy** in browser settings.

1. Disable **Prevent cross-site tracking**.

[Mobile version](https://support.apple.com/ru-ru/guide/iphone/iphb01fc3c85/ios):

1. Open **Settings** → **Safari**.

1. Go to **Privacy & Security** and disable **Prevent Cross-Site Tracking**.

### Mozilla Firefox {#firefox}

[Full version](https://support.mozilla.org/ru/kb/uluchshennaya-zashita-ot-otslezhivaniya-v-firefox-#w_kak-uznat-chto-blokiruetsia-na-saite_2):

Go to the page with the document in {{wiki-name}} and click ![](../../_assets/wiki/mozilla-security.png) and disable **Enhanced Tracking Protection for this site**.

[Mobile version](https://support.mozilla.org/ru/kb/zashita-ot-otslezhivaniya-v-firefox-dlya-android#w_izmenenie-urovnia-zashchity):

In the browser settings, disable **Enhanced Tracking Protection**.

### Google Chrome {#chrome}

[Full version](https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/2790761?co=GENIE.Platform%3DDesktop&hl=ru&oco=1):

1. In the browser settings, go to **Security and Privacy**, then click **Cookies and other site data** or follow the link `chrome://settings/cookies`.

1. Disable **Send a "Do Not Track" request with your browsing traffic**.

[Mobile version (Android)](https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/2790761?co=GENIE.Platform%3DAndroid&hl=ru&oco=1):

1. In the browser settings, select the section **Privacy and security** → **Do Not Track**.

1. Set the toggle to **OFF**.

### Yandex Browser {#ya}

[Full version](https://yandex.ru/support/browser/personal-data-protection/ytp.html#blocking-by-settings):

* Unblock a specific tracker:

   1. Click ![](../../_assets/wiki/ya-security.png) in SmartBox.

   1. To the right of the **sharepoint.com** and **yastatic.net** tracker, click ![](../../_assets/wiki/ico-actions.png) → **Unblock**.

* Allow trackers for all sites:

   1. In the browser settings, select **Sites** → **Advanced site settings**.

   1. In the "Cookies" section, select **Restrict third-party cookies**. This will only enable trackers for the sites that you have already visited.

[Mobile version](https://yandex.ru/support/browser-mobile-android-phone/privacy/ytp.html#unblocking):

1. Click ![](../../_assets/wiki/ya-security.png) in SmartBox.

1. Click "Site not working?" in Tracker blocking.

1. In the window that opens, click **Allow cookies**.

1. Refresh the page.
