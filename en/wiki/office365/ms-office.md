# Using Microsoft Office 365 documents

Office 365 documents stored on the corporate Sharepoint portal or in your personal OneDrive folder can be embedded in Wiki pages. This way, you can easily share documents, spreadsheets, and presentations and [co-edit them with your colleagues](co-edit.md). {{wiki-name}} supports Word, Excel, and PowerPoint documents. You can create documents directly from the {{wiki-name}} interface and edit them in-browser using Microsoft Office cloud services.

{% note alert %}

To create and edit embedded cloud documents, you must be [logged in to office.com](#office-login). If you don't have an Office 365 license, send a request via this [form](https://help.yandex-team.ru/?form=soft).

{% endnote %}

## Log in to Office 365 {#office-login}

To edit documents embedded in Wiki pages or create new cloud documents, log in to office.com using your Yandex corporate account:

1. Go to[https://www.office.com/](https://www.office.com/).

1. In the upper-right corner, click **Log in**.

1. Enter your corporate login, for example: `login@yandex-team.ru`.

1. If you have a license, you will be taken to the Office 365 home page.

   If you don't have the license, you will see the following message:
   ![](../../_assets/wiki/login-office.png)

   To get an Office 365 license, send an email to `help@yandex-team.ru`.

{% note info %}

External employees can also get an Office 365 license. An internal employee wishing to get a license for creating and editing documents must consult their supervisor.

{% endnote %}
