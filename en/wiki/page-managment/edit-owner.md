# Assigning page authors

A page can have one or more authors. By default, the author of the page is the user who created it.

{% note alert %}

Only the current author can assign other authors.

{% endnote %}

To assign another user as your page author:

1. Open a page.

1. In the upper-right corner of the page, click **Actions** and select **Settings**.

1. In the **Page Settings** window, click the line **Page authors** and specify the username of the new author of the page.

1. Click **Save**.
