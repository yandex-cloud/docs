# {{ yandex-cloud }} partner statuses

## Partner statuses {#statuses}

**{{ yandex-cloud }} Partner**: this partner status is awarded upon joining our partner program, completing online onboarding, and signing a partner agreement.

Advantages:

* Up to 25% commission per deal.
* Listing in the partner catalog.
* Grants for pilot projects.
* Presale assistance.
* Registration and protection of deals and projects.
* Access to training courses.
* Participation in partner events.

**{{ yandex-cloud }} Expert**: this status is for companies with an expert team, proven sales, and experience implementing projects in {{ yandex-cloud }}. [Learn more](./conditions.md) on how to earn this status.

This status includes all the **{{ yandex-cloud }} Partner** benefits, plus: 

* Up to 30% commission per deal.
* Access to leads.
* Marketing support.
* Technical Preview access to new tools and services.
* Dedicated {{ yandex-cloud }} architect.
* Dedicated partner manager.

**{{ yandex-cloud }} Professional**: this partner status is awarded to companies certified in one of the technology specializations. [Learn more](./conditions.md) on how to earn this status.

This status includes all the **{{ yandex-cloud }} Expert** benefits, plus: 

* Up to 35% commission per deal.
* Priority access to leads.
* Priority access to major projects.
* Partner architect support.
* Advanced co-marketing options.
* Strategic planning for partner business development.

## Types of technology specializations {#spec-types}

The **{{ yandex-cloud }} Professional** or **{{ yandex-cloud }} Expert** statuses include technology specializations aligned with {{ yandex-cloud }} key product areas.

### **Infrastructure & DevOps** {#infra-spec}

1. Migrating infrastructure to {{ yandex-cloud }}.
1. IT infrastructure configuration and deployment.
1. Creating multi-cloud or hybrid infrastructure.
1. Deploying business applications in {{ yandex-cloud }}.
1. Creating a microservice architecture.
1. Automated development environment.
1. Creating digital products and services for the customer.

### **Data Platform** {#platform-spec}

1. Enterprise data storage.
1. Machine learning for data analytics.
1. Real-time analytics.
1. Building a data lake and data lakehouse.
1. BI analytics.
1. Building transactional storage.

### **Security** {#secom-spec}

1. Network security.
1. Protecting websites and web applications.
1. Authentication and access management.
1. Cloud infrastructure security.

### **ML & AI** {#ml-spec}

1. Building smart voice assistants.
1. Call center automation.
1. Speech analytics of voice and text communication channels.
1. Creating multi-agent AI systems.

## Rewards for partners {#award}

Partners with the **{{ yandex-cloud }} Expert** and **{{ yandex-cloud }} Professional** status accrue [rebates](../terms.md#rebate) and [co-marketing budget](../terms.md#co-marketing):

#|
||  | **{{ yandex-cloud }} Expert** | **{{ yandex-cloud }} Professional** ||
|| **Infrastructure & DevOps** | 3.5% rebate and 1.5% co-marketing budget on paid consumption growth across [subaccounts](../terms.md#sub-account) and registered [projects](../program/var-tools.md#project-reg) | 7% rebate and 3% co-marketing budget on paid consumption growth across [subaccounts](../terms.md#sub-account) and registered [projects](../program/var-tools.md#project-reg) ||
|| **Data Platform** | ^ | ^ ||
|| **Security** | ^ | ^ ||
|| **ML & AI** | 3.5% rebate and 1.5% co-marketing budget on paid consumption of services within this specialization by [subaccounts](../terms.md#sub-account) | 7% rebate and 3% co-marketing budget on paid consumption of services within this specialization by [subaccounts](../terms.md#sub-account). ||
|#

Rebates and co-marketing budget accrue for each quarter the partner holds an active status. You can see your rebates under **Rebate accrual balance** on the [partner portal](https://partners.yandex.cloud/). 

For more information on using the co-marketing budget, see [Co-marketing program](../program/var-tools.md#co-marketing).

For more information on getting partner rewards, see the [Partner Agreement](https://yandex.ru/legal/cloud_partnership/?lang=ru).
