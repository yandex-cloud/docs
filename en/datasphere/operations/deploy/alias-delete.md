# Deleting an alias

1. {% include [find project](../../../_includes/datasphere/ui-find-project.md) %}
1. Under **{{ ui-key.yc-ui-datasphere.project-page.project-resources }}**, select **{{ ui-key.yc-ui-datasphere.common.alias }}**.
1. Click ![Options](../../../_assets/options.svg) next to the appropriate [alias](../../concepts/deploy/index.md#alias) and select ![Delete](../../../_assets/datasphere/delete.svg) **{{ ui-key.yc-ui-datasphere.common.delete }}**.
1. In the pop-up window, click **{{ ui-key.yc-ui-datasphere.common.submit }}**.
