# Data from Staff

In this block, the user can specify a Yandex employee or department. When typing text in the field, a hint is displayed.

## Block settings {#sec_settings}

### Question {#question}

Enter a field name or a prompt.

### Choice of data type {#data}

Select the data to show in this field:
* **Login** is employee's name or username

* **Group** is a department, service, or group.

* **Organization** is a Yandex business unit or legal entity.

* **Office** is the Yandex office.

### Several options {#multi}

Enable this option to allow multiple choice of employees or divisions.
