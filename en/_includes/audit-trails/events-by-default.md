In the management console, collection of some [data events](../../audit-trails/concepts/control-plane-vs-data-plane.md#data-plane-events) is on [by default](../../audit-trails/concepts/trail.md#default). Their delivery is billed as per the [pricing policy](../../audit-trails/pricing.md). If you do not need data events, disable their collection.
