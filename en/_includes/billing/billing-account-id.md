- Management console

   1. Open the {{ yandex-cloud }} [management console]({{ link-console-main }}).
   1. In the upper-left corner, click ![image](../../_assets/main-menu.svg) **All services**.
   1. Select ![image](../../_assets/billing.svg) [**Billing**]({{ link-console-billing }}).
   1. On the **Account list** page, select the account whose ID you would like to get.
   1. The billing account ID is displayed at the bottom of the **Overview** page under **General information**.
