* To enable maintenance at any time, select **{{ ui-key.yacloud.mdb.forms.value_maintenance-type-anytime }}** (default).
* To specify the preferred maintenance start time, select **{{ ui-key.yacloud.mdb.forms.value_maintenance-type-weekly }}** and specify the day of the week and the UTC time interval. For example, you can choose a time when the cluster is least loaded.

Both active and stopped clusters are subject to maintenance. Maintenance operations include DBMS updates, patches, etc.
