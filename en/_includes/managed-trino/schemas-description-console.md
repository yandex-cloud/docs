Optionally, in the **{{ ui-key.yacloud.trino.label_rbac-schema-access }}** field, specify the schemas the rule applies to:
* **{{ ui-key.yacloud.trino.rbac-catalog-match-by-name }}**: Select schema names.
* **{{ ui-key.yacloud.trino.rbac-catalog-match-by-name-regexp }}**: Enter a regular expression. The rule applies to the schemas whose names match the regular expression.
* **{{ ui-key.yacloud.trino.rbac-catalog-match-by-empty }}**: Rule applies to all schemas.
