To get notifications, the user must:

* Have the [monium.viewer](../../monium/security/index.md#monium-viewer) role for the folder with the alert.
* In the management console [settings]({{ link-console-main }}/settings):
    * Enable **{{ ui-key.yacloud_components.settings.label_monitoring-name }}**.
    * Depending on the notification method, specify an email address, phone number, Telegram or {{ messenger-full-name }} account.
