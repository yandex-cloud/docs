Under **{{ ui-key.yacloud.baremetal.title_section-network-interfaces }}** in the **Interface 1** section, select a [private subnet](../../../baremetal/concepts/private-network.md#private-subnet) in the [availability zone](../../../overview/concepts/geo-scope.md) where you are leasing the server.
