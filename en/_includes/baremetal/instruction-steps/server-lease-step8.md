Optionally, under **{{ ui-key.yacloud.baremetal.title_section-disk }}**, configure disk partitioning:

1. Click **{{ ui-key.yacloud.baremetal.action_disk-layout-settings }}**.
1. Specify partition settings. To create a new partition, click ![icon](../../../_assets/console-icons/plus.svg) **{{ ui-key.yacloud.baremetal.actions_add-partition }}**.

    To configure RAID arrays and disk partitions by yourself, click **{{ ui-key.yacloud.baremetal.action_destroy-raid }}**.
1. Click **{{ ui-key.yacloud.common.save }}**.
