# Managing a user account

[Organization](../../concepts/organization.md) users can manage their accounts in the [My account](../../concepts/my-account.md) portal. To manage your [cloud](../../../resource-manager/concepts/resources-hierarchy.md#cloud), [folder](../../../resource-manager/concepts/resources-hierarchy.md#folder), and service resources, as well as set up notifications and contact support, use the [management console](../../../console/quickstart/console.md).
