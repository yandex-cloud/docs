# Explaining the use of a semantic tag

To get an explanation of why a [semantic tag](../../../concepts/tags.md#sense-tags) triggered:

1. Open the {{ speechsense-name }} [home page]({{ link-speechsense-main }}).
1. Go to the space you need and select a project.
1. Navigate to the **{{ ui-key.yc-ui-talkanalytics.dialogs.dialogs }}** tab and find the dialog you need.
1. Open the dialog in one of the following ways:

    * To open a dialog preview, click the dialog line.
    * To open the dialog in a new window, click ![image](../../../../_assets/console-icons/arrow-up-right-from-square.svg) on the right in the dialog line.

1. On the page, find the semantic tags assigned to the dialog.
1. Click ![icon](../../../../_assets/console-icons/sense-tag-explain.svg) to the right of the tag.
1. The system will start generating the explanation for the semantic tag trigger. This will take some time.
   
   {% note info %}
   
   You can cancel the generation at any time.
   
   {% endnote %}

1. Once the explanation is generated, you will see an ![image](../../../../_assets/console-icons/sense-tag-explain.svg) icon with a red dot next to the tag.
1. Click the icon to view the explanation.
