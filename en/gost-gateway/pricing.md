## What goes into the cost of using {{ gost-gateway-name }} {#rules}

The cost of using {{ gost-gateway-name }} is based on the gateway model you select and is billed monthly.

You pay for the entire month regardless of when the billing starts. If you cancel your subscription, charges will stop from the first day of the next calendar month.

To submit a request to use the service, fill out [this form](https://forms.yandex.ru/surveys/13754806.0a628e54630cb46b090b20c5158aeb43c87cc412/). The billing starts within five days after you get a notification that your equipment is ready. To stop using the service, contact [support]({{ link-console-support }}).
