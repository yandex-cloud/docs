# Updating a dashboard

To update a dashboard:

1. Open Grafana at `https://grafana.sys.{{ cluster-domain }}` and log in with your username and password.
1. Open the **Dashboards** section.
1. Select the dashboard you want to update.
1. Click **Edit**.
1. Use the **Add** menu to add new visualizations or remove the existing ones.
1. Click **Save dashboard**.
