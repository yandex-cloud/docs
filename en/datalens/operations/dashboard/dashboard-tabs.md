# Configuring dashboard tabs in {{ datalens-full-name }}

You can [add](#tab-add), [rename, duplicate, and delete](#tab-rename-delete) tabs, [change their order](#tab-move) and [display](./dashboard-hide-tabs.md) on the dashboard, [select a reference tab](./dashboard-ai-reference-tab.md) for Neuroanalyst 2.0, as well as [configure the widget display order on a mobile device](./display-modes.md).

## Adding a tab {#tab-add}


{% include [datalens-workbooks-collections-select-note](../../../_includes/datalens/operations/datalens-workbooks-collections-select-note.md) %}


1. In the left-hand panel, click ![image](../../../_assets/console-icons/layout-cells-large.svg) **Dashboards** and select the dashboard you need. If you do not have a dashboard, [create one](create.md).


1. In the top-right corner, click **Edit**.
1. Click ![image](../../../_assets/console-icons/gear.svg) at the top of the screen. The settings window will open on the right.
1. Click the **Tabs** field or the ![image](../../../_assets/console-icons/sliders-vertical.svg) icon next to it.


1. In the window that opens, click **Add**.
1. Click **Save**.
1. In the top-right corner, click **Save**.


## Rename, duplicate, or delete a tab {#tab-rename-delete}


1. In the top-right corner, click **Edit**.
1. Click ![image](../../../_assets/console-icons/gear.svg) at the top of the screen. The settings window will open on the right.
1. Click the **Tabs** field or the ![image](../../../_assets/console-icons/sliders-vertical.svg) icon next to it.


1. In the window that opens, click ![image](../../../_assets/console-icons/ellipsis.svg) next to the tab name and select an action.
1. Click **Save**.
1. In the top-right corner, click **Save**.

## Changing the tab order {#tab-move}


1. In the top-right corner, click **Edit**.
1. Click ![image](../../../_assets/console-icons/gear.svg) at the top of the screen. The settings window will open on the right.
1. Click the **Tabs** field or the ![image](../../../_assets/console-icons/sliders-vertical.svg) icon next to it.


1. In the window that opens, drag the tabs to arrange them in the order you need.
1. Click **Save**.
1. In the top-right corner, click **Save**.
