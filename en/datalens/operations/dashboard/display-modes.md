# Widget display order on a mobile device

To set up the order of charts and selectors in the mobile version or in mailing lists:


1. In the top-right corner, click **Edit**.
1. Click ![image](../../../_assets/console-icons/gear.svg) at the top of the screen. The settings window will open on the right.
1. Click the **Tabs** field or the ![image](../../../_assets/console-icons/sliders-vertical.svg) icon next to it.


1. In the window that opens, next to the tab name, click ![image](../../../_assets/console-icons/ellipsis.svg) → **Widget order**.
1. In the window that opens, set the order of widgets in the mobile version.
1. Click **Apply** and then click **Save**.
1. In the top-right corner, click **Save**.
