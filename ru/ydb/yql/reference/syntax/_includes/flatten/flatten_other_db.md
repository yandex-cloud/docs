### Аналоги FLATTEN BY для других СУБД {#flatten-other-dmb}

* PostgreSQL: `unnest`;
* Hive: `LATERAL VIEW`;
* MongoDB: `unwind`;
* Google BigQuery: `FLATTEN`;
* ClickHouse: `ARRAY JOIN / arrayJoin`;
